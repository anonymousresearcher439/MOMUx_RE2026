# Alert Management Requirements

This page captures requirements related to how system alerts are generated, presented, interpreted, and shared among operational roles during mission execution. These requirements address observed breakdowns in alert detectability, alert overload, alert attribution, and the ability of operators to understand the meaning and significance of alerts.

Alerts play a critical role in supporting safe and effective supervision of autonomous operations by signaling conditions that may require attention or intervention. The requirements in this section focus on ensuring that alerts are salient, interpretable, credible, and visible so that operators can recognize, understand, and respond appropriately to important events without being overwhelmed or misled.
